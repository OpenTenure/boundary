Welcome to SOLA Community Server

Using SOLA Community server you can enrol as a Community Member and view recordings made by community members. User enrolment can be done online and once you complete enrolment the Community Technologist will review it and approve. Upon approval a confirmation code will be sent to your e-mail address. You will then need to activate your enrolment request, by following the received link. Another way is to contact your Community Technologist and they will enroll you as a Community Server user.

All Community Member enrolments are subject to review and approval by the Community Server Technologist and the SMSMCL project team.

If you are already enrolled, use Login page to login into the system.
